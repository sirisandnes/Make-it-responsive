WHO ARE WE?

OUR STORY

What do we think when we hear the word innovation? Renewal? Modernization? Change? Essencially, it is new products, services or production processes that are central when we talk about innovation. Or one can look at the concept as bringing about changes in the way economic goods or other values are produced. INOVA is a company that works with economy and reproduction of products. We in the company are concerned with bringing in recycled products, and then innovating it into something useful in everyday life.

OUR WORK

INOVA works for a safe and sustainable workplace. We want to form innovation groups where you can contribute to creating more environmentally friendly products. These products go through our market, where we as a company manage our own finances and marketing. As the innovation groups have created a new stable product, most of the income goes to this group. The company makes sure to market and launch further to the world. Both we benefit from it, and you who work benefit from it. Are you the right person for our team? Contact us!
